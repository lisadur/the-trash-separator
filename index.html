Dispose trash into the correct bin!

Welcome to Germany! Make your landlord happy and contribute to recycling trash by allocating it into the correct bins. Use the arrow keys on your keyboard! You can only fail once, so be careful.

  • Green bin: Organic waste*
  • Yellow bin: Plastics / packaging
  • Blue bin: Paper
  • Black bin: Residual waste

*Note: Organic waste bins in your area may be brown or green.
